✨ SPELL SPREAD

Create logical phrases where the first letters of each word spell out a given word

How to Play

You'll be given a word. Your job is to create a logical phrase or sentence where the first letter of each word spells out the given word. The phrase should relate to the meaning of the word!

Rules

  • The first letters of your words must spell the given word in order.
  • Your phrase should be meaningful and relate to the word's meaning.
  • It can be a definition, advice, saying, or universal truth.
